How GrubSaver Works

Our simple process connects those with excess food to those who can use it, creating a more sustainable food system for everyone.

User Registration & Login

New users sign up as either Buyer (NGO/Biogas/Individual) or Seller (Restaurant/Donor).

Post Food

Sellers upload food details — name, type, quantity, image, and location — to make it available for request.

Browse & Order

Buyers explore listed food based on location and availability, then place an order request.

Complete Transaction

Food gets a second life instead of going to waste, and feedback builds trust in our community.
